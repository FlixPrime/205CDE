ABOUT US

Founded in 2017, in order to do our part for the environment, we use 100% reclaimed wood to make our decks, thus removing the need to chop down trees and damaging Mother Earth even further. You might be skeptical and think "Oh, there must be some sort of gimmick associated with using reclaim wood." To which our response is NO! There's nothing exceptionally different from using fresh cut down wood in fact using reclaimed wood might be even better!
